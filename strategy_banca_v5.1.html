    1. Business Objective

    Identify opportunity, optimize cost, drive campaigns, monitor performance.

    2. Core Metrics

    Penetration = Policies / Customers
Digital Share = Digital Policies / Total Policies
Weighted Loss Ratio = Σ(Premium × LR) / Σ Premium
COA per Policy = Cost / Policies

    3. Predictive Logic

    Score =
 (1 - Penetration)*0.4
 + CASA*0.3
 + (1 - Digital)*0.3

    4. Campaign Logic

    IF Penetration < 20% → Cross-sell
IF Digital < 20% → Digital push
IF Loss Ratio > 80% → Risk control

    5. COA Logic

    Premium = Base × (1 + COA × Elasticity)
Elasticity ≈ 0.5

    6. Monitoring Metrics

    Conversion = Policies / Leads
COA per policy
Premium growth
